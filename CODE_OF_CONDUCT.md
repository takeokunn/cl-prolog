# Code of Conduct

This project follows a concise contributor conduct policy focused on technical,
evidence-based collaboration.

## Our Standards

Participants are expected to:

- argue from concrete evidence
- keep criticism technical and specific
- respect review boundaries and repository policies
- avoid harassment, discrimination, and personal attacks

Participants must not:

- publish private security reports before maintainers respond
- derail technical discussion with repeated bad-faith behavior
- use sexualized language or imagery in project spaces
- intimidate or target contributors personally

## Scope

This policy applies to repository discussions, issues, pull requests, review
comments, and other project-managed communication channels.

## Enforcement

The project maintainer is responsible for interpreting and enforcing this
policy in project-managed spaces. The maintainer may edit, hide, lock, or
remove content that violates it, and may warn, temporarily restrict, or revoke
participation when repeated or severe violations make productive collaboration
impractical. Enforcement decisions should be proportionate to the behavior,
its impact, and any established pattern of misconduct.

When reporting a conduct problem, include:

- where it happened
- the specific message or behavior
- why it blocks safe collaboration
- any immediate moderation need

For a confidential report, use a private contact method explicitly published
on the [maintainer's GitHub profile](https://github.com/takeokunn). If the
conduct occurred on GitHub and no such contact method is available, use
GitHub's private [reporting abuse or spam](https://docs.github.com/en/site-policy/acceptable-use-policies/github-appeal-and-reinstatement#how-to-report-abuse)
process. Do not put sensitive personal information in a public issue.

The maintainer will limit report details to people who need them to investigate
and act, except where disclosure is required for safety or by law. Reports will
be reviewed for scope and evidence, the affected parties may be asked for
clarification, and the reporter will receive an outcome when a private reply
channel is available. A person affected by an enforcement decision may request
one reconsideration through the same private channel, identifying the decision
and any material information that was missed. The maintainer's decision after
reconsideration is final for project-managed spaces.

Security-sensitive reports must follow [`SECURITY.md`](SECURITY.md) instead.
